Solid Hardwood Flooring

There is nothing that replicates the warmth of solid timber underfoot. Our hardwood flooring is sourced from the same premium wood species we use across all our joinery: teak, honne, and red sal. Milled and finished to your specification.

Materials

Available Wood Species

All flooring and railings are available in your choice of wood species. Each timber is inspected and naturally seasoned before production.

01

Burma Border Teak

The premium choice for flooring. Dense, tight-grained, and exceptionally durable. Resistant to moisture, termites, and heavy foot traffic.

02

Ivory Coast Teak

Comparable durability to Burma teak at accessible pricing. An excellent choice for large floor areas where performance and value both matter.

03

Honne

Dense, stable, and resistant to warping. A popular choice for flooring in Bangalore's climate. Its reddish-brown tone deepens and enriches over time.

04

Red Sal

A cost-effective structural hardwood with good load-bearing properties. Well suited for utility areas and secondary spaces where budget is a priority.
