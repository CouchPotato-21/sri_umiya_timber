Materials

Available Wood Species

All frames are available in your choice of wood species. Each timber is inspected on arrival and naturally seasoned before production.

01

Burma Border Teak

The benchmark hardwood for Indian joinery. Dense, straight-grained, and highly resistant to moisture and termites. The premium choice for main entrance frames.

02

Ivory Coast Teak

A robust hardwood with excellent structural properties. Comparable durability to Burma teak at more accessible pricing. Well suited for large volume projects.

03

Tanzania Teak

Fine-grained with a uniform warm tone. Machines cleanly for detailed profiles. A strong choice where both aesthetics and performance matter.

04

Thailand Teak

Fine-grained with a uniform golden-brown tone. Machines cleanly for detailed profiles and decorative joinery where aesthetics are as important as strength.

05

Honne

A dense, reddish hardwood prized for its stability and clean finish. Widely used in door and window frames for its resistance to warping.

06

Red Sal

A cost-effective structural hardwood with good load-bearing properties. Often specified for internal frames and utility openings.

07

Mahogany

Medium-density with a rich, reddish-brown grain. Takes finishes exceptionally well. A preferred choice when visual grain quality matters.
