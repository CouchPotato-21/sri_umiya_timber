Our Story

"Four decades of working with wood teaches you things no specification sheet can."

Sri Umiya Timber's roots go back to 1983, when our founder established one of Bangalore's earliest timber mills. That heritage of deep material knowledge, hands-on craftsmanship, and earned trust has shaped everything we do.

In 2001, we expanded into a full-service timber workshop, moving from raw material supply into precision manufacturing of door frames, window frames, shutters, and custom joinery. Today, from our 2,400 sq ft facility in Banashankari, we serve homeowners and builders across Karnataka.
