Our Services

CNC Carving

Any design. Any detail. Carved with precision.

What We Do

Precision Meets Craft

Our CNC machines can carve any 2D or 3D design into wood with exact precision. From intricate traditional motifs to clean geometric patterns, every design is executed with the same level of detail.

Customers can bring their own design, share a reference image, or choose from hundreds of our preset designs. Our team will engineer it and carve it to perfection.

CNC Carved Doors

Custom Orders

The Process

How It Works

01

Share Your Design

Send us a reference image, sketch, or choose from our preset designs. We will confirm feasibility and suggest any adjustments.

02

We Engineer It

Our team converts your design into CNC machine instructions. You get a confirmation of dimensions and design before production begins.

03

Carved and Delivered

The piece is carved, finished, and delivered to your site. Standard lead time is 15 working days from order confirmation.
